Research

The REST Lab studies how sleep and non-pharmacological interventions influence pain and related health outcomes.

Sleep & Pain

We examine bidirectional relationships between sleep and chronic pain, focusing on mechanisms that maintain symptoms over time.

  • Daily diary and EMA designs
  • Wearable sleep assessment (e.g., actigraphy)
  • Clinical and functional pain outcomes

Open-Label Placebo & Expectations

Our work explores how expectations and learning processes influence symptoms, including open-label placebo interventions.

  • Randomized and factorial trials
  • Expectation manipulation
  • Mechanistic and clinical outcomes
